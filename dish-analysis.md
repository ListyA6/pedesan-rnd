# Pedesan Concept — Cross-File Dish Analysis

Based on 40 recipes: 20 from `babat-madura.md` + 20 from `babat-jeroan-nusantara.md`.
Compiled: 2026-05-05.

## PART 1 — INGREDIENT FREQUENCY

### ALWAYS (90%+ of recipes — the non-negotiables)

| Ingredient | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| Bawang merah (shallots) | 40/40. Always heavy ratio — typically 6–12 butir per 300–500g protein. |
| Bawang putih (garlic) | 40/40. Always in bumbu halus. Ratio usually ½ of bawang merah volume. |
| Garam | 40/40. |
| Minyak goreng | 40/40. Both for tumis bumbu and deep frying. |
| Sambal (in some form) | ~38/40. Structural — not garnish. Sambal korek, terasi, dadak, balado, ungkep. The dish is designed to be eaten with it. |

---

### OFTEN (50–85% of recipes — the flavoring system)

| Ingredient | Approx. Frequency | Notes |
|------------|------------------|-------|
| Ketumbar (coriander) | ~30/40 (75%) | The warm-savory anchor of the bumbu. Without this the dish tastes flat. Ground or whole then blended. |
| Kemiri (candlenut) | ~28/40 (70%) | Gives richness and helps bumbu coat/adhere to protein surface. Usually sangrai first. |
| Daun jeruk (kaffir lime leaf) | ~31/40 (78%) | Aromatic, slightly citrus. Goes in both the pre-boil and ungkep stages. |
| Daun salam (Indonesian bay leaf) | ~31/40 (78%) | Primarily deodorizing function — removes offal smell. Less about flavor, more about smell management. |
| Serai/sereh (lemongrass) | ~26/40 (65%) | Aromatic layer, geprek form. Also deodorizes. Works alongside daun salam. |
| Kunyit (turmeric) | ~26/40 (65%) | Color and earth note. Often bakar (charred) for deeper flavor. The yellow is the visual signature. |
| Jahe (ginger) | ~29/40 (72%) | Dual role: deodorizer in pre-boil, flavor in bumbu halus. Heavy in Madura recipes. |
| Lengkuas (galangal) | ~25/40 (63%) | Earthiness + subtle pine note. Often geprek not halus. More prominent in Sunda and Padang styles. |

| Ingredient | Approx. Frequency | What It Signals |

| Daun pandan | ~1/40 | Odor neutralizer, rare. |

## PART 2 — DISH DNA

### What makes this dish this dish — regardless of the protein

**1. Double-cook structure is non-negotiable.**
Rebus/ungkep WITH bumbu → goreng. The boil is not just tenderizing — it is flavor infiltration. The liquid must reduce to nearly nothing before frying. Protein that is just boiled and fried (without the bumbu-in-liquid reduction step) is a different dish.

**2. The bumbu lives inside the protein, not on it.**
Unlike battering or marinading, the ungkep method pushes flavor into the fiber as the liquid concentrates. By the time frying happens, the protein is already fully seasoned inside. The frying only adds crust — it adds no flavor of its own.

**3. The fried exterior is thin and caramelized, not battered.**
The residual bumbu on the protein surface caramelizes in hot oil, creating a thin, slightly sticky-crispy crust. No coating is needed. This is the textural signature: dense-chewy interior + thin caramelized exterior.

**4. Sambal is structural, not optional.**
The ungkep-fried component is deliberately restrained — mild enough that it doesn't overwhelm. The sambal provides the acid, heat, and aromatic freshness the dish is designed to receive. They are one system, not two.

**5. The deodorizing pair is infrastructure, not flavor.**
Daun salam + jahe (and serai) appear in almost every pre-boil or first boil. Their function is odor management for the offal. They do not define the final flavor. You can change or omit them after the deodorizing stage without changing the dish identity.

**6. The flavoring pair is the real DNA.**
Ketumbar + kemiri = the dish's specific warm-savory character. Ketumbar provides the aromatic baseline. Kemiri provides body and helps bumbu adhere. If you remove both, you still have boiled-and-fried protein — but it no longer tastes like this dish.

**7. Kunyit + heavy bawang putih = color and savory backbone.**
The yellow tint is the visual marker across all 40 recipes. Bawang putih in heavy ratios gives the foundational savoriness. These two never disappear.

**8. Finishing textures are regional identity markers, not dish-definers.**
- Serundeng = Madura/JaTim identity
- Kremes = urban JaTeng warung identity
- Bumbu hitam (kluwek/petis) = deep Madura identity
- Balado/sambal hijau siram = Padang identity
- Sambal korek/bawang = Jawa warung universal identity

Any finishing can go on top of the same base system. The base is the dish. The finishing is the brand.

**9. The protein is a vehicle, not the dish.**
The bumbu system (ungkep-susut-goreng + sambal) works on any dense protein that survives double cooking: babat, paru, iso, limpa, hati, and by extension tofu, tempe, jackfruit, or any non-offal protein with similar structural density. What makes it "pedesan" is the method + bumbu logic, not the cut.

**10. Time and patience is the technique.**
The ungkep stage cannot be rushed — water must reduce completely so bumbu adheres and concentrates. The goreng must hit stable-temp oil so the crust caramelizes evenly. There are no shortcuts that preserve the identity of the dish. Presto shortens tenderizing only; the ungkep-susut stage still takes its time.

## PART 3 — 4 EXPERIMENTAL RECIPES

### How to use these:
Each recipe has a **Hypothesis** — what you're actually testing. After cooking, note: did the hypothesis hold? That's the R&D loop.

---

### EXPERIMENT 1 — Bumbu Hitam Kluwek Siram (Does kluwek actually work on babat?)

**Hypothesis:** Kluwek bumbu hitam — which is culturally bound to bebek/ayam — will create a more complex, earthy depth on babat than the gula merah or petis shortcut versions. The siram method (pour sauce over fried babat) keeps the fried texture while adding the dark layer.

**Protein:** Babat sapi 500g (presto 30 mnt)

**BUMBU HITAM KLUWEK (base — buat banyak, simpan):**
- 8 bh bawang merah
- 4 bh bawang putih
- 150g kluwek, kupas, rendam air panas 10 mnt
- 4 bh kemiri, sangrai
- 1 sdt ketumbar, sangrai
- 1/2 sdt jinten, sangrai
- 1 ruas kunyit
- 1 ruas jahe
- 3 bh cabe merah keriting (bisa dibuang bijinya)
- 1 sdt garam
- 1 sdm gula merah, sisir

Blender semua halus. Masak tanpa minyak di atas api sedang sambil diaduk hingga air habis. Masukkan 2 sdm minyak, tumis terus hingga bumbu harum, matang, dan berwarna sangat gelap. Simpan di toples, tahan kulkas 30+ hari.

**UNGKEP BABAT (standar):**
- 500g babat presto (30 mnt)
- 7 bh bawang merah, 5 bh bawang putih → haluskan
- 1 sdt ketumbar, 2 btr kemiri → haluskan
- 1 ruas kunyit
- 3 lembar daun jeruk, 2 lembar daun salam, 1 btg serai
- 1/2 sdt garam, 1/2 sdt gula merah
- 300ml air

Tumis bumbu halus + daun-daunan hingga harum. Masukkan babat + air. Masak hingga air susut habis. Angkat, potong 3x3cm.

**FINISHING:**
1. Goreng babat dalam minyak panas sedang hingga kecoklatan (jangan sampai kering). Angkat, tiriskan, tata di piring.
2. Panaskan 3 sdm bumbu hitam kluwek + 3 sdm air kaldu (atau air panas). Aduk hingga cair dan panas.
3. Siram di atas babat goreng.
4. Tambahkan serundeng kelapa di pinggir piring sebagai kontras tekstur.
5. Sajikan dengan sambal korek (rawit + bawang putih, ulek kasar, siram minyak panas).

**Yang dites:** Apakah kluwek menambah kompleksitas rasa yang tidak bisa digantikan petis/gula merah? Apakah siram-style lebih baik dari ungkep-in-kluwek untuk babat?

### EXPERIMENT 2 — Kremes Air Kelapa + Double Crunch (kremes + serundeng di satu piring)

**Hypothesis:** Kremes (rice flour crunch) dan serundeng (toasted coconut crunch) adalah dua jenis crunch yang berbeda karakter — kremes ringan-airy, serundeng dense-earthy. Dikombinasikan di satu piring harusnya saling melengkapi, bukan saling ganggu.

**Protein:** Babat atau paru sapi 500g

**UNGKEP (dengan air kelapa — dari resep #8 Madura):**
- 8 bh bawang merah, 6 bh bawang putih → haluskan
- 1 sdm ketumbar bubuk, 1 sdm kunyit bubuk, 4 btr kemiri sangrai → haluskan
- 2 sdm asam jawa tanpa biji
- 2 keping gula merah kecil, iris
- 1 liter air + 800ml air kelapa (kunci resep ini)
- 3 btg sereh, 6 lbr daun salam
- Garam secukupnya

Tumis bumbu halus + daun-daunan hingga kering dan harum. Pindah ke panci/presto bersama babat, air kelapa, air, gula merah, asam jawa, garam. Presto 30 mnt. Tiriskan — pisahkan babat dan sisa cairan ungkep.

**SERUNDENG (dari sisa bumbu ungkep):**
- 150g kelapa parut
- Sisa bumbu ungkep (1/2 cangkir)
- 1 sdm gula pasir (agar cepat kering)

Campur kelapa parut + bumbu ungkep + gula pasir. Masak di api sedang sambil terus diaduk hingga kering dan kecoklatan. Simpan terpisah.

**KREMES (teknik Finna Food):**
- 100g tepung beras
- 1 sdm tepung tapioka
- 1 butir kuning telur
- 200ml air kaldu sisa ungkep babat
- Sejumput baking powder

Campur semua. Masukkan ke botol aqua yang tutupnya dilubangi. Panaskan minyak banyak dan stabil (180°C). Tuang adonan dari ketinggian ±30cm dengan gerakan memutar. Biarkan hingga keemasan, angkat, tiriskan.

**GORENG BABAT:**
Goreng babat ungkep dalam minyak panas hingga kecoklatan. Angkat.

**PLATING:**
Babat goreng → taburi kremes → taburi serundeng di pinggir → sambal korek atau sambal terasi segar di sisi.

**Yang dites:** Apakah dua jenis crunch bekerja bersamaan? Mana yang lebih kuat secara rasa — kremes atau serundeng? Perlu disajikan terpisah atau memang enak dicampur?

### EXPERIMENT 3 — Petis Udang + Asam Jawa Wet Finish (The Dark Sauce Without Kluwek)

**Hypothesis:** Petis udang Madura + asam jawa + sedikit kecap manis bisa menghasilkan finishing gelap dan kompleks yang mendekati bumbu hitam kluwek tanpa menggunakan kluwek — lebih cepat, lebih accessible, dan punya profil rasa yang unik (lebih umami-fermented vs. earthy-kluwek).

**Protein:** Babat sapi 300g

**UNGKEP STANDAR:**
- 6 bh bawang merah, 4 bh bawang putih → haluskan
- 1 sdt ketumbar, 3 btr kemiri → haluskan
- 1 ruas kunyit, 1 ruas jahe
- 3 lbr daun jeruk, 2 lbr daun salam, 1 btg serai
- 1/2 sdt garam, 300ml air
Masak hingga susut habis. Goreng babat hingga kecoklatan. Sisihkan.

**SAUS PETIS-ASAM (finishing layer):**
- 1 sdm petis udang (petis Madura, bukan petis ikan)
- 2 sdm air asam jawa (dari 1/2 sdt asam + 2 sdm air panas)
- 1 sdt kecap manis
- 3 bh bawang merah, iris halus
- 2 bh cabe merah, iris serong
- 5 bh cabe rawit merah utuh
- 1 sdm minyak bekas menggoreng babat

Panaskan minyak. Tumis bawang merah + cabe sampai layu. Masukkan petis, aduk. Tuang air asam + kecap manis. Masak hingga mendidih dan sedikit kental. Koreksi rasa (seharusnya: gelap, asam, gurih, sedikit manis — tidak pedas dominan). Masukkan babat goreng, aduk rata sebentar. Angkat.

**Sajikan dengan:** nasi putih panas, lalapan mentimun + kemangi, sambal terasi mentah di sisi.

**Yang dites:** Apakah profil petis-asam cukup kuat untuk jadi "signature sauce" tanpa kluwek? Berapa rasio petis:asam yang paling balance? Apakah perlu tambahan gula merah atau sudah cukup dari kecap manis?

### EXPERIMENT 4 — Rica-Rica Finishing pada Base Ungkep Kuning (Madura base + Manado finish)

**Hypothesis:** Bumbu ungkep kuning standar (Madura/JaTim base) yang difinishing dengan rica-rica Manado (cabai merah keriting + rawit + tomat + banyak daun jeruk + jeruk nipis) akan menciptakan profil rasa yang lebih complex dan segar daripada sambal korek biasa — dan bisa menjadi "house sambal" untuk konsep pedesan.

**Protein:** Paru sapi 500g (bisa diganti babat atau iso)

**UNGKEP PARU:**
- 7 bh bawang merah, 5 bh bawang putih → haluskan
- 1 sdt ketumbar, 3 btr kemiri sangrai, 2cm kunyit bakar → haluskan
- 2 lbr daun salam, 3 lbr daun jeruk, 1 btg serai
- 1/2 sdt asam jawa, 1/2 sdm gula merah
- 1 sdt garam, 400ml air

Rebus paru dulu 30 mnt, buang air. Iris 0,75cm. Tumis bumbu, masukkan paru + air, masak hingga air susut habis. Goreng paru dalam minyak panas hingga kecoklatan-renyah. Angkat, tiriskan.

**RICA-RICA FINISHING SAUCE:**
- 10 bh cabe merah keriting
- 1 genggam cabe rawit merah
- 2 bh tomat merah ukuran sedang, potong kasar
- 15 siung bawang merah
- 8 siung bawang putih
- 2 cm jahe, geprek
- 3 btg serai, geprek
- 8 lbr daun jeruk
- 40ml air jeruk nipis (kunci — harus terasa segar)
- 1 sdt garam, 1 sdt kaldu jamur
- 3 sdm minyak

Blender kasar cabe merah + rawit + tomat + bawang merah + bawang putih (jangan terlalu halus, biar bertekstur). Panaskan minyak. Tumis serai + jahe geprek hingga wangi. Masukkan blenderan cabe, masak hingga "pecah minyak" (minyak terpisah di pinggir, ±10-15 mnt). Masukkan daun jeruk. Tambahkan garam + kaldu. Matikan api. Masukkan air jeruk nipis, aduk. Koreksi rasa.

Siram di atas paru goreng, atau sajikan terpisah di mangkuk kecil.

**Yang dites:** Apakah rica-rica bisa berdiri sebagai "house sambal" konsep ini — cukup segar untuk balance dengan offal yang berat, cukup kuat untuk tidak tenggelam? Berapa mnt tumis optimal sebelum jeruk nipis masuk? Apakah lebih baik siram di atas atau sajikan terpisah?
